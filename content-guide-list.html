Guide List

A guide lists gathers multiple guides under a single system component. Guides may be ordered manually or dymanically by subject, user, or group. You may also wish to add a header (<h4>) above the guide list.

Requirements – Manual

Follow these steps to ensure the minimum requirements when adding your guide list.

Manually-selected Guides
0 Click on the Add / Reorder   dropdown and select Guide List.
1 Select Manually Selected Guides from the List type dropdown.
2 Select a guide to add to your list from the Selection(s) dropdown. Repeat this step until you have added all required guides.
3 Click Save
Add a Rich Text / HTML content item above your guide list with a descriptive header, such as "Related Engineering Guides," etc.
