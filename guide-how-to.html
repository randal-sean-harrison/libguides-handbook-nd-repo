How-to Guides

How-to guides should be structured according to the best practices of instructional design, moving users through a process toward completion of specific goals. Examples might include:

  • Helping users to work in the R language to perform statistical analysis for economics research
  • Helping users to understand the whys and hows of managing scholarly citations
  • Providing participants of the Hesburgh Library Hackathon with logistics and other resources related to the event
