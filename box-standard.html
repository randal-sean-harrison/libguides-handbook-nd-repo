Standard Box

The most frequently used type of box, standard boxes are made up of stacked chunks of closely-related content. Your content should be stacked so that users may naturally scroll vertically down through your content. You may use the hierarchy of headings to further delineate these sections. It is also possible to use Rich Text Boxes to divide your content into multiple columns.

While concealing your content in tabbed boxes may be useful in certain limited cases, stacking content vertically on the page is recommended.

System Components
  • Inserted via the Add / Reorder   dropdown
  • Span the entire width of the box
  • May not be broken into columns
  • Books
  • Databases
  • Links
  • Guide Lists
  • Documents
  • RSS Feeds
Features
  • Are code snippets inserted into the Source Window of Rich Text / HTML content items
  • Can usually be divided into multiple columns.
  • Cards
  • Collapsible Panels
  • Code Wells
  • Lists
  • images and icons
  • videos and widgets
