RSS Feed

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) feeds push updates on recent content from blogs and other Web media providers. Most significant contributors of online content provide an RSS feed and you can embed these directly into your guides. For example, you may wish to read the latest 5 updates from the Museum of Modern Art, or perhaps NASA's various feeds.

Feed Format Examples

  • http://rss.nytimes.com/services/xml/rss/nyt/Technology.xml
  • http://www.moma.org/feeds/exhibitions.rss
