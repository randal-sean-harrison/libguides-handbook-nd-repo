Guide Requirements

Internal Guides are for use by Library personnel only and are not to be shared with the public. As such, they are not bound by limitations on number of columns or particular style requirements.

Good Use Case
A training guide for employees of a particular department or team, connecting them with resources and knowledge to help them do their work, is suited to an Internal Guide.

Requirements At-a-glance
0 May be based on either the Internal Guide (1 column) or the Internal Guide (1-4 column) templates.
1 Must remain Private, hidden from users who don't have the URL, and not published to the patron-facing list of guides.
3 May require password protection for sensitive information.

Guide Variants

We recommend the useful constraints of the Internal Guide (1 column) template over the Internal Guide (1-4 column) template, though needs will vary.
