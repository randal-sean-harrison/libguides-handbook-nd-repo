Learning Opportunities

We offer both regularly scheduled Training and a monthly Guide Clinic (community-of-practice brown-bag luncheon) where we work collectively and collaboratively on our guides. See the calendar below for details and to register.

Workshops
LibGuides 101 · Introduction to the LibGuides Platform

A general introduction to the LibGuides platform, including general best practices working with system content (e.g., adding books, databases, etc.). Those brand new to LibGuides or needing a refresher are encouraged to attend this workshop.

LibGuides 102 · LibGuides Best Practices

For those familiar with the LibGuides platform, this workshop will reinforce policies, best practices, e.g. writing for the web, etc. and introduce ways to leverage ‘Bootstrap’, the mobile-friendly User Interface (UI), features set baked into LibGuides.

LibGuides 200 · Introduction to the LibGuides Platform

Advanced customizations of LibGuides, including working with LibGuides widgets and other APIs.

Community of Practice
  Guide Clinic
Bring your LibGuides and your lunch! In this brown bag community of practice, we work together as equals on guides, with assistance available by the trainers who host the event.
