Video

Video is highly engaging and facilitates active learning, allowing users multiple views of material at their own pace.

  • Video Builder
  •  Back to top

Video Builder

The LibGuides platform does not allow for uploading of video. However, video can be embedded from any number of streaming services, including YouTube, Vimeo, The Internet Archive, TED, and any others using the <iframe> embed. Remember that as librarians we must be models of academic honestly when reusing content. While embedding a link to a video shared on another platform reduces potential liability, it remains our responsibility to follow the University's policies on copyright and intellectual property.

Embed Size
Make sure to choose an embed size of 640 pixels for the video you wish to embed.
